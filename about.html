About School #37

Founded in 1936, we are one of the oldest and most prestigious schools in Samarkand, dedicated to providing excellence in education with a specialized focus on English language learning.

Our Journey Through Time

Eight decades of educational excellence and community service

1936

Foundation

School #37 was established in the heart of Samarkand, becoming one of the first specialized educational institutions in the region. Built with a vision to provide quality education to the community.

1960s-1980s

Growth & Development

The school expanded its facilities and curriculum, establishing itself as a leading educational institution. New buildings were added and the student population grew significantly.

1990s

Independence Era

Following Uzbekistan's independence, the school adapted to new educational standards while maintaining its commitment to excellence. English language specialization was strengthened.

2000s-Present

Modern Excellence

Embracing modern educational technologies and methodologies, School #37 continues to produce outstanding graduates who attend top universities worldwide.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

We foster our students' love for learning, encourage them to try new and exciting things, and give them a solid foundation to build on. Our vision is to develop well-rounded, confident, and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

Vision

We will do this by providing a welcoming, happy, safe, and supportive learning environment in which everyone is equal and all achievements are celebrated. We believe that a happy child is a successful one.

"We are committed to providing a positive, safe and stimulating environment for children to learn, where all are valued. We intend that all children should enjoy their learning, achieve their potential and become independent lifelong learners."

Explore our modern facilities designed for 21st-century learning

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest educational technology, including interactive whiteboards, multimedia systems, and comfortable seating arrangements that promote collaborative learning.

  • Interactive smart boards and projectors
  • High-speed internet connectivity
  • Flexible seating for group work

Library & Resource Center

Our comprehensive library houses thousands of books, digital resources, and study materials. It's a quiet haven for learning and research, supporting students in their academic journey.

  • Over 10,000 books and periodicals
  • Digital research databases
  • Quiet study areas and reading rooms
  • Computer workstations for research

Science Laboratories

Our state-of-the-art science labs provide hands-on learning experiences in physics, chemistry, and biology. Students conduct experiments and develop scientific thinking in a safe, modern environment.

  • Modern laboratory equipment
  • Safety equipment and protocols
  • Specialized subject labs
